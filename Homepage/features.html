All Features

Here are the major features for Customer, Veterinarians, and Admin dashboard

Account Login

The users are required to create their accounts before using the application.

Profile

The user can update or edit their profile information.

Appointment

The customer can make an appointment through the system. The Veterinarians and Admins can view or edit the customer's appointment.

View Information

The customer can view available services, Veterinarians Directory, and their pet's Medical record. The Veterinarians and Admins can also view the customer information and their choice of service.

Transaction

The customer can make a payment through online or over the counter. Transaction history can be viewed by Customer and Admins

Medical Records

Every medical record of the pet will be save on the system

Contact

Customers can contact the clinic, and vice versa

Reports

Consists of all of the data that have been recorded
